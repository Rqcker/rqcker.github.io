About

Junhao Song,

I am currently a postgraduate student in Applied Computational Science and Engineering at Imperial College London supervised by Dr. Rhodri Nelson. I received my BSc in Computer Science (Artificial Intelligence) from Heriot-Watt University in 2023. My Bachelor Honors Dissertation (Optimisation and Application of Symbiotic Relationship-Based Artificial Immunity Systems for Real-World Problems in Computational Intelligence) was completed under the supervision of Dr. Wei Pang and Dr. James McKinna. My third-year large-scale project was completed and supervised by Prof. Ron Petrick.

My research covers a wide range of topics in deep learning and computational intelligence, and I am committed to developing general-purpose methods that are applicable to solving real-world problems. My current research focuses on building mathematical models to learn representations and develop solutions from the real world.
