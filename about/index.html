About

Junhao Song,

Last Updated: 20th October, 2024

He is currently pursuing a postgraduate degree in Applied Computational Science and Engineering at Imperial College London. He completed his BSc (Hons) in Computer Science (Artificial Intelligence) at Heriot-Watt University in 2023. During his undergraduate studies, he benefitted greatly from the invaluable guidance and support of Prof. Wei Pang and Prof. Ron Petrick.

His research interests lie in the fields of multimodal learning and computational intelligence. His work focuses on self-supervised learning in human-inspired computing and the optimisation of model inference using evolutionary computation. He is also investigating the application of diffusion models in video generation and exploring self-supervised learning with large language models. His research aims to enhance the coherence and quality of audio-visual content by integrating autoregressive models with multimodal data.
