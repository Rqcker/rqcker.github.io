Junhao Song

I am currently a postgraduate student in Applied Computational Science and Engineering at Imperial College London. My supervisor is Dr. Rhodri Nelson. I received my BSc in Computer Science (Artificial Intelligence) from Heriot-Watt University in 2023. My undergraduate honors dissertation, titled Optimisation and Application of Symbiotic Relationship-Based Artificial Immunity Systems for Real-World Problems in Computational Intelligence, was completed under the supervision of Dr. Wei Pang and Dr. James McKinna. Additionally, my third-year project was conducted under the guidance of Prof. Ron Petrick.
My research spans various topics in the field of deep learning and computational intelligence. I am dedicated to developing general-purpose methods that can effectively address real-world problems. Currently, my research focuses on building mathematical models to learn representations and develop solutions derived from real-world data.
